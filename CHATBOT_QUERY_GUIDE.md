## âš ï¸ Troubleshooting

### "Unable to fetch energy data"
- Check you're logged in
- Ensure at least one hub is connected
- Verify SSR is turned ON

### "No hubs found"
- Go to Settings
- Add your hub's serial number
- Hub will auto-link to your account

### "Price not calculated"
- Go to Settings
- Set your price per kWh
- All costs will update automatically

### "System offline"
- Check hub connection
- Turn on SSR (main breaker)
- Wait for data to refresh
